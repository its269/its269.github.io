Kelin Graphics System proudly presents the Xline Eco-Solvent Large Format Printer – a perfect blend of cutting-edge technology, eco-friendly solutions, and cost-efficiency designed to meet the high demands of signage, outdoor displays, and indoor graphics production.

Why Choose an Eco-Solvent Printer?

Eco-solvent printers use a refined solvent-based ink formulation that is less aggressive than traditional solvent inks, making them a greener alternative without compromising durability or print quality. They are especially ideal for indoor and outdoor applications, offering water and scratch resistance, UV stability, and vivid color reproduction on a variety of media types.
