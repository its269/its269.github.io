KIRIN - SUBLIMATION PRINTER LARGE FORMAT

DA182T Plus

High-speed Roll to Roll Dye Sublimation Digital

Key Features: 8 Kyocera industrial print heads, print speed up to 650m²/h



Product Overview

The DA182T Plus is a high-speed roll-to-roll dye sublimation digital textile printer equipped with 8 Kyocera industrial print heads, achieving print speeds up to 650m²/h. The dye sublimation printing process significantly improves printing speed, ensures coherent and exquisite pattern splicing, and delivers more gorgeous colors. This excellent printing quality enhances the consumer experience and empowers industry users to achieve a win-win situation.

Superior Print Head for Higher Productivity

Meeting the demands of huge daily order volumes requires equipment with high productivity. The DA182T series textile printer utilizes industrial high-speed Kyocera print heads and maximizes their output through a special combination and control system, leading to improved efficiency.

Combination of Quality and Speed: High Definition Output

Through its unique drive design and ink supply system, the DA182T series printing machines can use disperse ink, high-temperature disperse ink, etc., to realize more accurate ink droplet injection, resulting in image-level print quality. Furthermore, the automatic cleaning and moisturizing system reduces the risk of print head blockage, improving output stability and enabling single-person operation. The large-capacity floor-mounted ink supply system significantly reduces ink replacement frequency and supports printing on thin paper (minimum 31g/㎡), thereby lowering material loss and printing costs.
